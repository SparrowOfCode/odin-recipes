Rosemary Bread

Description

A bread recipe that both smells and tastes fantstic. Its moist, light and has a crispy crust.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chopped rosemary
  • 1 1/4 salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups room temp water
  • 2 tablespoons cornmeal

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, garlic, rosemary, salt, pepper, and yeast.
  2. Using a wooden spoon or your hands, add water and mix until a wet, sticky dough forms, about 30 seconds.
  3. Cover bowl tightly with plastic wrap and let stand at room temperature until surface is dotted with bubbles, about 18 to 24 hours.
  4. Lightly oil a 10-inch cast iron skillet or coat with nonstick spray; sprinkle with cornmeal.
  5. Working on a lightly floured surface, gently shape dough into a round shape.
  6. Place dough into the prepared skillet. Cover with a clean dishtowel and let stand at room temperature untill dough has doubled in size and does not readily spring back when poked with a finger, about 2 hours
  7. Preheat oven to 450 degreesF. Place into oven and bake until golden brown, about 30-40 minutes.
  8. Serve warm.
